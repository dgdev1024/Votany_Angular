Votany - A Place To...
Voice Your Issues

Here at Votany, you can create and manage your own polls in order to voice whatever issue you would like to voice, from "What's your favorite rock band?" to "Who do you think shoud win the election?". Use your imagination!

Voice Your Vote

Here at Votany, you can view and cast a vote on a wide range of issues put forth by other users. You don't even need to be registered in order to vote, either!

If you do register, however, you can also expand upon those issues by adding your own choices to polls. Don't see your favorite choice? Add it!

Voice Your Opinion

Here at Votany, if you register an account, you can also expand upon your votes by posting comments on your favorite polls, and engage in an additional conversation on the issues you vote on.
